Filipa Calado is a student, teacher, and writer based in New York City. Her Ph.D. research explores how digital tools and environments change the way we read and study literature. Her teaching focuses on Queer and Latinx literature and the Digital Humanities at NYU and Hunter College, CUNY. See her CV.
